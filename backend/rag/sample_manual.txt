FACTORY MACHINERY MAINTENANCE MANUAL
Model Series: CNC-2000 / LATHE-500 / PRESS-H1 / WELD-ARC
Revision: 4.2 | Maintenance Division

================================================================================
SECTION 1 — NORMAL OPERATING PARAMETERS
================================================================================

1.1 CNC Machine (CNC-01, CNC-02)
  Temperature       : 60°C – 80°C (spindle bearing zone)
  Vibration         : 0.5 – 2.0 mm/s RMS
  Spindle RPM       : 1400 – 1600 RPM (standard stock removal)
  Coolant pressure  : 6.0 – 8.0 bar

  Warning thresholds:
    Temperature > 85°C      → inspect coolant flow and bearing lubrication
    Temperature > 100°C     → immediate shutdown, bearing failure suspected
    Vibration > 2.5 mm/s    → check tool balance and spindle preload
    Vibration > 5.0 mm/s    → emergency stop, structural damage risk
    RPM drop < 1200         → check drive belt tension and motor brushes
    RPM drop < 900          → motor fault or drive inverter failure

1.2 Lathe Machine (LATHE-01, LATHE-02)
  Temperature       : 55°C – 78°C
  Vibration         : 0.3 – 1.8 mm/s RMS
  Spindle RPM       : 1400 – 1600 RPM
  Cutting pressure  : 6.5 – 8.5 bar

1.3 Hydraulic Press (PRESS-01)
  Temperature       : 50°C – 75°C (hydraulic fluid)
  Vibration         : 0.2 – 1.5 mm/s RMS
  System pressure   : 6.0 – 8.0 bar
  Warning: Pressure < 5.0 bar → seal degradation or pump wear
  Warning: Pressure < 3.0 bar → immediate shutdown, hydraulic failure

1.4 Welding Station (WELD-01, WELD-02)
  Temperature       : 40°C – 70°C (transformer housing)
  Vibration         : 0.1 – 1.0 mm/s RMS

================================================================================
SECTION 2 — FAULT DIAGNOSIS
================================================================================

2.1 Overheating (Temperature > 85°C)

  Probable causes (in order of likelihood):
    A. Coolant flow blockage — clogged filter or kinked hose
    B. Spindle bearing wear — insufficient lubrication
    C. Cutting tool dulled — generating excess friction heat
    D. Coolant pump failure — no active cooling
    E. Ambient temperature spike — check HVAC

  Diagnostic steps:
    1. Check coolant flow at nozzle — should be 4–6 L/min
    2. Inspect coolant filter (Part: PN-FILT-003) — replace if pressure drop > 0.5 bar
    3. Listen for bearing noise — grinding or squealing indicates bearing failure
    4. Measure coolant temperature at inlet vs outlet — delta > 20°C = blockage
    5. Check coolant pump pressure: should read 6–8 bar

2.2 Excessive Vibration (Vibration > 2.5 mm/s)

  Probable causes:
    A. Unbalanced cutting tool or worn tool holder
    B. Spindle bearing preload lost — bearing must be replaced
    C. Loose machine base bolts — check torque specification
    D. Worn drive belt — check belt deflection

  Diagnostic steps:
    1. Perform tool runout check — should be < 0.01mm
    2. Manually rotate spindle — roughness indicates bearing damage
    3. Check base mounting bolts — torque to 120 Nm (M16 bolts)
    4. Inspect drive belt for cracking and fraying (Part: PN-BELT-001)

2.3 RPM Deviation (RPM < 1200 or > 1800)

  Probable causes (low RPM):
    A. Drive belt slipping — check tension and condition
    B. Motor brushes worn — carbon deposit buildup
    C. Inverter drive fault — check drive display for error code
    D. Spindle load too high — reduce depth of cut

  Probable causes (high RPM):
    A. Speed controller fault
    B. Feedback encoder failure

2.4 Pressure Loss (Pressure < 5.0 bar)
  Probable causes:
    A. Hydraulic seal degradation (Part: PN-SEAL-004)
    B. Pump wear — measure pump output at bypass
    C. Internal leak — inspect hose fittings and valve seats
    D. Hydraulic filter clogged (Part: PN-FILT-003)

================================================================================
SECTION 3 — REPAIR PROCEDURES
================================================================================

3.1 Spindle Bearing Replacement

  Required parts:
    - Ball Bearing 6205 (Part: PN-BEAR-002) × 2
    - Oil Seal 40x60x10 (Part: PN-SEAL-004) × 1
    - Grease: SKF LGMT 3, 10g per bearing

  Safety: LOCKOUT / TAGOUT before starting. Allow machine to cool below 40°C.

  Steps:
    1. Remove spindle cover (4× M8 bolts, 22 Nm torque).
    2. Loosen draw bar and remove tool holder.
    3. Remove spindle nose nut — LEFT-HAND THREAD, torque 180 Nm.
    4. Use bearing puller to extract front bearing — do NOT hammer.
    5. Clean bearing housing with lint-free cloth and isopropyl alcohol.
    6. Press new bearing (PN-BEAR-002) using bearing press — apply force to outer race only.
    7. Pack bearing with 10g of LGMT 3 grease.
    8. Replace oil seal (PN-SEAL-004) — lip faces inward.
    9. Reassemble spindle nose nut — torque to 180 Nm LEFT-HAND THREAD.
    10. Replace spindle cover — torque M8 bolts to 22 Nm in cross pattern.
    11. Run spindle at 500 RPM for 10 minutes — monitor temperature.
    12. Verify vibration < 1.5 mm/s before returning to production.

3.2 Coolant Filter Replacement

  Required parts:
    - Hydraulic Filter HF10 (Part: PN-FILT-003) × 1

  Steps:
    1. Shut down machine and depressurise coolant system.
    2. Place drip tray beneath filter housing.
    3. Unscrew filter housing (counterclockwise) — 60 Nm torque.
    4. Remove old filter element and dispose of properly.
    5. Clean housing interior with lint-free cloth.
    6. Insert new filter element (PN-FILT-003).
    7. Refit housing — torque to 60 Nm.
    8. Restart coolant pump and check for leaks.
    9. Verify coolant pressure returns to 6–8 bar.

3.3 Drive Belt Replacement

  Required parts:
    - Drive Belt A40 (Part: PN-BELT-001) × 1

  Steps:
    1. Isolate power — LOCKOUT / TAGOUT.
    2. Remove belt guard (2× M6 bolts, 10 Nm).
    3. Loosen motor mounting bolts to relieve belt tension.
    4. Slide old belt off pulleys.
    5. Fit new belt (PN-BELT-001) — do NOT use tools to lever onto pulley.
    6. Adjust motor position for correct tension: 10mm deflection under 50N force.
    7. Retighten motor mounting bolts — 45 Nm.
    8. Refit belt guard — 10 Nm.
    9. Run machine at low speed for 5 minutes and recheck tension.

3.4 Hydraulic Seal Replacement

  Required parts:
    - Oil Seal 40x60x10 (Part: PN-SEAL-004) × 2

  Steps:
    1. Depressurise hydraulic system completely — bleed valve on accumulator.
    2. Drain hydraulic fluid into clean container.
    3. Remove cylinder end cap (8× M10 bolts, 75 Nm).
    4. Extract piston rod carefully — support with padded V-blocks.
    5. Remove old seals from piston groove — use plastic tool only.
    6. Clean piston rod with lint-free cloth — inspect for scoring.
    7. Lubricate new seals (PN-SEAL-004) with clean hydraulic fluid.
    8. Fit seals — lip must face pressure side.
    9. Reassemble cylinder — torque end cap bolts to 75 Nm in star pattern.
    10. Refill hydraulic fluid and bleed system.
    11. Test at full pressure — verify no leaks.

================================================================================
SECTION 4 — TORQUE SPECIFICATIONS SUMMARY
================================================================================

  Spindle nose nut (M40 LEFT-HAND)   : 180 Nm
  Spindle cover bolts (M8)           : 22 Nm
  Motor mounting bolts (M12)         : 45 Nm
  Belt guard bolts (M6)              : 10 Nm
  Machine base bolts (M16)           : 120 Nm
  Coolant filter housing             : 60 Nm
  Hydraulic cylinder end cap (M10)   : 75 Nm

================================================================================
SECTION 5 — PREVENTIVE MAINTENANCE SCHEDULE
================================================================================

  Daily:
    - Check coolant level and colour
    - Inspect belt for visible damage
    - Log temperature, vibration, RPM at startup

  Weekly:
    - Clean coolant filter strainer
    - Check hydraulic fluid level
    - Verify all safety guards in place

  Monthly:
    - Replace coolant filter (PN-FILT-003) if pressure drop > 0.5 bar
    - Lubricate spindle bearings
    - Check drive belt tension

  Quarterly:
    - Replace drive belts (PN-BELT-001)
    - Full bearing inspection
    - Hydraulic fluid analysis

  Annually:
    - Full spindle overhaul
    - Replace all seals (PN-SEAL-004)
    - Electrical insulation test
